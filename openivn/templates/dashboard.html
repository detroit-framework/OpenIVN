About
What is DETROIT?

DETROIT is a platform designed to facilitate third-party access to vehicular data.

Wait a second, isn't Detroit the name of a city?

Indeed, Detroit is the name of the largest and most populous city in Michigan. However, DETROIT stands for Data Collection, Translation, and Sharing for Rapid Vehicular App Development.

What does DETROIT have to do with OpenIVN?

OpenIVN is the name of software running as DETROIT's backend. The IVN in OpenIVN is short for Intra-Vehicular Network.


Register New App
Step 1

Register an app by clicking on Register New App in the sidebar.

Step 2

Fill out the form to provide the following information about the app:

  • App name
  • A description of the app's purpose. This will be displayed to users so they may determine if they'd like to provide share their data with your app.
  • Select whether you'd like the data to be made available for download or streamed in near real-time to a specified endpoint.
  • Select which data permissions you'd like to request users' approval. (See the permissions table at the bottom of this page for details.)
Step 3

Submit your app.

Step 4

DETROIT will review your submission shortly. You will be informed of your app's status via the email registered with your account.
